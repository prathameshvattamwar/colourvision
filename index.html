Color vision deficiencies affect many people worldwide. Here, you can learn more about different types of color blindness and find tips to better distinguish colors.

For example, individuals with red-green color blindness may find it helpful to use apps and tools that highlight contrast and color differences. Technology advancements also provide glasses designed to enhance color perception.

Understanding your condition and practicing regularly with exercises can improve your ability to differentiate between colors over time.
